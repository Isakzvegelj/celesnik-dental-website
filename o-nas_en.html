History & Legacy

Dr. Emil Čelesnik — Pioneer of Aesthetic Porcelain

The founder of our clinic and one of the first private dentists in Slovenia. His vision shaped aesthetic dentistry as we know it today.

dr. Nina Čelesnik
Continuing the legacy of dr. Emil Čelesnik

Founded in 1971

Dr. Emil Čelesnik founded our dental clinic in Bled in 1971, becoming one of the first private dentists in Slovenia. At a time when private dental practice was still in its infancy, Dr. Emil demonstrated exceptional courage and vision. He introduced advanced aesthetic porcelain techniques to our region and laid the foundation for modern aesthetic dentistry that is now considered the standard.

An Artistic Vision — Imitating Nature

For Dr. Emil Čelesnik, dentistry was not just medicine — it was art. His fundamental philosophy was that every tooth created by a dentist must be a perfect reproduction of nature. He did not seek mere function — he sought absolute aesthetics, teeth that are not only healthy but truly beautiful. Each crown, each bridge, each veneer was for him a work of art that must be indistinguishable from a natural tooth. He summarized this vision in his famous saying: "Art is the reproduction of reality."

International Exhibitions and Recognition

Dr. Emil's works astonished the international professional community. In 1986, at the congress in Belgrade, he presented an extraordinary reconstruction of a human skull with porcelain teeth — a work that demonstrated his ability to perfectly imitate natural dental anatomy. In 1989, at the international exhibition in Nuremberg, he presented his most famous masterpiece: a porcelain tooth with a golden snake motif, inscribed with "UMETNOST JE REPRODUKCIJA RESNIČNOSTI" (Art is the reproduction of reality). This work became a symbol of his artistic approach to dentistry.

International Impact — Especially in Japan

Dr. Emil's works transcended the borders of Slovenia. His aesthetic porcelain techniques attracted the attention of experts worldwide, particularly in Japan, where aesthetic precision and craftsmanship are highly valued. His works were presented at international congresses and publications, contributing to the international recognition of Slovenian aesthetic dentistry.

Practice in Opatija, Croatia

In addition to his work in Bled, Dr. Emil Čelesnik also practiced in Opatija, Croatia, where he served international patients with his knowledge and artistic approach. This international experience enriched his approach and further strengthened his reputation as a leading expert in aesthetic dentistry in the region.

The Practice Today

Dr. Nina Čelesnik — Clinic Director

The rich legacy of dr. Emil Čelesnik is today continued with the same love, precision, and aesthetic sense by his daughter.

Continuing the Family Legacy

Dr. Nina Čelesnik is the daughter of the founder dr. Emil Čelesnik and shares his deep dedication to aesthetics and dental excellence. With over 20 years of experience, she is regarded as one of the leading experts in aesthetic smile reconstructions and complex prosthetics in Slovenia. She regularly collaborates with top European specialists and is a member of the International Team for Implantology (ITI).

Her philosophy is the same as her father's: your smile should not only be healthy, but truly enchanting. Dr. Nina combines her father's artistic legacy with the latest technologies and approaches — from digital smile design to minimally invasive techniques.

Specialization in fixed prosthetics and aesthetic porcelain veneers
